Auto Roboti Car

Description

Auto Roboti Car (ABC): A team of 2-3 members will be given 90 minutes to assemble a car and program the car to follow a given track. The basic arduino code will be provided. The team will be graded based on three criteria assembly, programming and lap time with the total score of 30 marks

Pre requisite

Basic arduino coding

Team size:

Max. Team of 5 and Min. of 2 members

Event location:

Sensorics Lab, II floor, Block B
