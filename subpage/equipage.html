Equipage

Summary

Stage 1:Hydraulic Arm

Design and build a light simple wheel based hydraulic lifting system that is able to lift a weight of 300 grams.

Participants have to prepare the models at home a few weeks prior to the event.

Stage 2: Criar

Inspired by the actual design of tower crane, this stage is about designing a tower crane, the structure must be made only from Popsicle sticks and adhesive, which consist of a vertical mast and a lever-arm.
