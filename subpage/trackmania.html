Track Mania 3.0

Description

Track Mania with its first edition starting with the batch of 2017 has been one of the most successful events organized by the departments and is now back at Technovanza 2019. In this event the participants need to assemble a R/C car in the shortest period. These assembled cars will be raced on an obstacle course designed by one of our finest. The participants will be judged on their timing across the track.
