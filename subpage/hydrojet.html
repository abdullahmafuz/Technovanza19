Hydrojet 4.0

Description

Hydrojet is back at Technovanza 2019 with its 4th consecutive edition. Hydrojet is an event where contestants design a water propelled plastic bottle which is lifted and launched by means of a launcher made of PVC pipes with the help of air pump. This event helps in bringing out the design characterisitics in team. The particpants would be judged based on the distance covered by the rocket, altitude achieved and design of the rocket and the launcher.

Pre requisite

Skills for solving common puzzles, Radio Tuning

Team size:

Max. Team of 4 and Min. of 3 members

Event location:

Hostel Ground

Pre requisite

The launcher and the bottle need to be made prior to the event with respect to the guidelines provided.
