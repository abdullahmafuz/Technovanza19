Laser Blaster

Description

The players are given a laser shooting gun by which they need to shoot total of 3 targets within a time limit of 2 minutes. Each team has 15 shots. The targets are placed in different places and each one activates after 40 secs. The targets are split into 3 parts with bullseye rewarding 2 points and other 2 rewarding 1.5 and 1 each

Pre requisite

Stable hands, Calm Mind and Eagle Eyes

Team size:

Max. Team of 5 and Min. of 2 members

Event location:

B-308, 2nd floor, B-Block
