FIFA 19

Description

FIFA 19 is a football simulation video game in the FIFA series. FIFA 19 is a sports game that simulates association football. The game features 52 fully licensed stadiums from 12 countries, including new stadiums, plus 30 generic fields for a total of 82. All 20 Premier League stadium are represented in the series.

RULES:

  1. We will be accepting at max 32 players.
  2. No 2 on 2 matches, only 1 on 1 valid.
  3. Players can select any team of their choice (no world XI or classic XI).
  4. Initially the halves will be of 5 minutes. For Quarters, Semis, and Final the halves will be of 6 minutes.
  5. In case of draw, we will directly go into penalties. For Quarters, Semis, and Final we will have extra time and then followed by penalties.
  6. No changes in the club squad will be entertained. You will have to play with inbuilt squads.
  7. 2 minutes will be given to configure the controllers. You can bring your own controllers and keyboards.

Team size:

Single
